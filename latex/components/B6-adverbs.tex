\subsection{\ruby{副詞}{ふく|し}と\ruby{接続詞}{せつ|ぞく|し} (adverbs and conjunctions)}
Adverbs modify both verbs and adjectives. They may also modify entire noun phrases or sentences.


\subsubsection{Grammatical}
\begin{center}
\centering
\resizebox{\linewidth}{!}{%
% Help: \multicolumn{2}{c}{}, \multirow{2}{*}{}, cmidrule(l){3-5}
\begin{tabular}{@{}lll@{}}
    \toprule
    \textbf{Modifier} & \textbf{Meaning} & \textbf{Notes} \\
    \toprule
    \ruby{例}{たと}えば & for example/for instance & \\
    つまり & in short/in other words & (\ruby{詰}{つ}まり) \\
    \midrule
    まず & firstly & (\ruby{先}{ま}ず) \\
    \ruby{次}{つ}いで & secondly/next/subsequently & also a \conjunction \\
    <cause>だから[ですから]<result> & therefore (to speaker, <result> is a natural consequence of <cause>; strong expression of speaker's attitude) & \conjunction; [polite], also an expression; \href{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DSYc2BQrJEY}{[MCJ]} \\
    <cause>それで<result> & (objective そ) therefore (general cause-and-effect; <result> must have certainly happened (either past or now)) & \conjunction; \href{https://dictionary.goo.ne.jp/thsrs/16809/meaning/m0u/}{[goo]}, \href{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DSYc2BQrJEY}{[MCJ]} \\
    <cause>そこで<result> & (objective そ) therefore (<cause> is problem/situation, <result> is action taken to solve/improve/advance) & \conjunction; \href{https://dictionary.goo.ne.jp/thsrs/16809/meaning/m0u/}{[goo]}, \href{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DSYc2BQrJEY}{[MCJ]} \\
    <cause>すると<effect> & thereupon (<effect> happens \emph{immediately} after <cause>) & \conjunction; \href{https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DSYc2BQrJEY}{[MCJ]} \\
    % & & \\
    \bottomrule
\end{tabular}%
}
